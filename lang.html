LANGUAGES IN INDIA

India as a country is diverse and multilingual. There is no language called “Indian”.India speaks hundreds of languages and dialects. Some are extinct, while some are still in use with considerable speakers. To have a rough idea of India's linguistic distribution, here is a map:

The top 11 languages of India according to total speakers are:

  1. Hindi — 551.4 Million
  2. English — 125.3 Million
  3. Bengali — 91.1 Million
  4. Telugu — 84.9 Million
  5. Marathi — 84.1 Million
  6. Tamil — 66.7 Million
  7. Urdu — 59.1 Million
  8. Kannada — 50.7 Million
  9. Gujarati — 50.2 Million
  10. Odia — 36.6 Million
  11. Malayalam — 33.7 Million
